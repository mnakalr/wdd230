Windhoek Chamber of SMEs Site Plan

Site Name and Purpose

Site Name: Windhoek Chamber of SMEs. This name was selected to reflect the focus on supporting SMEs in Windhoek, emphasizing local growth and digital marketing strategies.

Site Purpose: To act as a hub for SMEs in Windhoek, offering resources, networking opportunities, and strategies for leveraging social media for business growth.

Scenarios

  • What social media marketing workshops are available this quarter?
  • How can I connect with other local SMEs for partnership opportunities?
  • Where can I find case studies on successful SMEs in Windhoek?

Color Schema and Typography

Color Schema: Blueish-Green (#32a89b) for headings and accents, Light Gray (#f2f2f2) for backgrounds, and Dark Gray (#4e555b) for text.

Typography: 'Roboto' for body text and 'Lato' for headings, providing clarity and a modern aesthetic.

Wireframe

A rough sketch of the homepage layout is provided for both mobile and desktop views, highlighting the placement of key elements like navigation, content sections, and CTAs.
